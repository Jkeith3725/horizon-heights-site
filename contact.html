Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services, delivery times, and coverage areas.

How fast do you deliver real estate photos?

We deliver standard residential photo galleries within 48 hours of the shoot. Rush delivery is available for urgent listings.

Are your drone pilots licensed?

Yes, all our pilots are FAA Part 107 certified and we carry full liability insurance for every flight. We prioritize safety and compliance with all airspace regulations.

Do you travel outside Marietta?

Yes! We serve the entire Mid-Ohio Valley including Parkersburg, Vienna, Williamstown, Athens, and St. Marys. We travel to your location (mileage rates may apply).

Can you manage my business social media?

Yes, we offer monthly social media packages that include content creation, video reels, and daily management for Facebook and Instagram. We handle the posting schedule so you can focus on running your business.

Do you offer video tours for homes?

Yes, our Cinematic Walkthroughs and Drone Tours are perfect for showing property layout and flow. We can even add voiceovers to highlight key features.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Cash, Credit Card, Venmo, and Zelle. Invoices can be paid online securely.
