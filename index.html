What is Horizon Heights Productions?

Horizon Heights Productions is the premier provider of drone services, professional photography, and aerial media in Marietta, Ohio and the Mid-Ohio Valley. While we specialize in real estate photos and video, our FAA-certified pilots also deliver cinematic 4K footage for commercial projects, large land mapping, events, and social media content. Whether you need a single property shoot or a full-scale production, we elevate your vision with safety, precision, and speed.

About Horizon Heights

Horizon Heights Productions blends aviation precision with creative storytelling. From flight planning and FAA-compliant operations to post-production polish, we protect your reputation, safeguard client data, and deliver visuals that respect the privacy of every property we fly.

We collaborate with Realtors, developers, main street businesses, tourism bureaus, and event organizers to craft media that feels intentional, safe, and on-message. Every project is scoped with safety briefings, square footage realities, and scheduling needs in mind so your team stays confident from kickoff to delivery.
