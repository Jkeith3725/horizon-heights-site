About Horizon Heights

Horizon Heights Productions blends aviation precision with creative storytelling. From flight planning and FAA-compliant operations to post-production polish, we protect your reputation, safeguard client data, and deliver visuals that respect the privacy of every property we fly.

We collaborate with Realtors, developers, main street businesses, tourism bureaus, and event organizers to craft media that feels intentional, safe, and on-message. Every project is scoped with safety briefings, square footage realities, and scheduling needs in mind so your team stays confident from kickoff to delivery.

What to Expect — The Horizon Heights Process

From the first call to delivery day, every project follows a transparent roadmap so you know exactly what is happening and when your assets will arrive.

01

Discovery Call

We learn the story, MLS deadlines, travel considerations, and necessary permits. You receive a clear scope with pricing and mileage.

02

Pre-Flight Planning

Shot lists, weather windows, airspace checks, and client prep guides are confirmed. Stakeholders get a schedule + asset checklist.

03

Production Day

Interior, exterior, and aerial coverage happen with redundant storage and on-site previews to ensure accuracy before wrap.

04

Assets arrive via online gallery with MLS-ready, social, and print folders. Need tweaks or reels? We handle edits within 24 hours.
