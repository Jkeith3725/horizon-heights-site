Here is how to put your changes on the internet in **3 simple steps**.

### Step 1: Make Your Changes
Open your files (like `index.html`) and make your edits. Save them when you are done.

### Step 2: Run the Publisher
1. Open your folder: **HorizonHeightsWebsite**
2. Look for the file named: **`Publish Website.command`**
3. **Double-click** it.

*(A black window will pop up. That is the "Terminal" working for you!)*

### Step 3: Tell It What You Did
1. The window will ask you to type a message.
2. Type a few words about what you changed (example: *"Added new video"* or *"Fixed typo"*).
3. Press the **Enter** key on your keyboard.
