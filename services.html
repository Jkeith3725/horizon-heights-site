Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I get my real estate photos delivered?

Photo galleries are delivered within 48 hours of the shoot. Drone films, reels, and social edits follow within 3–5 business days, with rush delivery available for active listings.

What areas do you serve in Ohio?

We're based in Marietta, Ohio and service the entire Mid-Ohio Valley including Parkersburg, Athens, and surrounding areas. Travel is billed at $0.70 per mile from downtown Marietta.

Are you FAA certified and insured?

Yes. Horizon Heights maintains FAA Part 107 certification, full liability insurance, and can provide Certificates of Insurance (COI) upon request.

How much does drone photography cost for real estate?

Our most popular package is the Residential Bundle at $675, which includes interior & exterior photos plus a cinematic drone tour. Individual photo packages start at $275.

Do you offer social media management for real estate agents?

Yes. We offer monthly social media packages starting at $700/month for Facebook and Instagram, up to $2,800/month for full-platform management including YouTube and TikTok.

Can you film large land parcels and farms?

Absolutely. Our Large Land Coverage service is $200 per parcel plus $10 per acre, and includes cinematic drone film with narrated overview, boundary overlays, and unlimited photos.
